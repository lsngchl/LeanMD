# Kernel of the Bent-Chain Mixed Hessian

## Lemma

Assume the hypotheses of the bent-chain proposition, and put
\[
e_j:=z_{j+1}^0-z_j^0,
\qquad
u_j:=\frac{e_j}{|e_j|},
\qquad 1\le j\le2m-1.
\]
Let
\[
H:=(\Phi_{1,m})_{YX}(X^0,Y^0).
\]
Then
\[
\operatorname{rank}H=md-1.
\]
Moreover,
\[
\ker H
=
\operatorname{span}\{(u_1,0,\ldots,0)\},
\]
and
\[
\ker H^T
=
\operatorname{span}\{(0,\ldots,0,u_{2m-1})\}.
\]
